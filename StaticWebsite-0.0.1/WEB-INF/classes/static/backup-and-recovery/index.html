Backup And Recovery

Home > Services > Backup And Recovery

Keeping your business up and running without downtime

The ability to recover and back up your IT systems and data infrastructure cannot be overstated – it is literally one of the most important layers of protection any serious business needs to have to operate normally.

Whether your company has a good backup and recovery plan can make a difference between growing your business and succeeding, and losing business and failing in the eyes of consumers and competition. If we look at organizations that remain consistently successful amid many economic and world events that could critically jeopardize their business, one thing is obvious – consistency and continuity.

But what does this mean for you? The answer is simple – it means keeping your business up and running without the downtime and undeterred, despite any adverse circumstance. Business continuity instills a sense of trust of a consumer in your products and services, and creates loyalty and ultimately, revenue.

The benefits of operating optimally and without downtime are difficult to overstate, and one of the main things that need to be in place to ensure this is a good backup and recovery plan.

A well-formulated backup and recovery plan predicts and mitigates scenarios such as:

  • Theft of devices
  • Theft of data
  • Data corruption
  • Malicious threats and attacks
  • Natural disasters (fires, floods, earthquakes)
  • The electrical outage caused issues
  • Data lost by accident

As previously mentioned, the ability to recover and back up your IT systems and data infrastructure cannot be overstated – it is literally one of the most important layers of protection any serious business needs to have to operate normally. Even a downtime of a few minutes can lead to damages to your business and revenue and loss of trust in the industry.

What can you do now? We have a backup solution that will keep your business running 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Under our Backup and Recovery package, we at TechProComp offer the following services:

  • Local Backup (NAS, SAN)
  • Local and Cloud Backup (Infrastructure, Workstations)
  • Automatic backup that does not slow down your network
  • Instant Restore
  • Windows 365 backup
  • G Suite Backup
  • AWS Backup
  • Azure Backup
  • Website Backup
  • Reporting

Our team of experts will identify key points that we will mark as “risk areas” and work on a plan to prevent any issues for you. We will create a list of priorities and essential systems for your business and go from there – creating a perfect action plan for your needs. This action plan will encompass a hierarchy of systems and recovery policies assigned to each, ordered by how essential they are to run your business. To create this list, we will complete a consultation session with you.

When it comes to pricing, that will depend on your storage size and the robustness of your systems and infrastructure already in place. To get your free estimate, please contact us. We will complete a full analysis of your data infrastructure and all IT systems and complete a full consultation with you outlining the initial steps you need to take to protect your business.

Should you choose to move forward with us, we also offer a routine checkup and testing of all your systems, which we will perform on the schedule you perform. These tests will ensure the integrity of your systems at all times and help us keep identifying potential risk areas and create plans as we go.

Lastly, we assure you that our pre-agreed pricing will be honored and that there will never be any hidden costs – your priorities are our priorities!
